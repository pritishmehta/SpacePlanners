Racking FAQ

Common Questions About Industrial Racking

Find technical answers about our heavy-duty and modular racking solutions.

What is the load capacity of your heavy-duty racks?

Our industrial racking systems are rated to support loads up to 10 tons per level, depending on the configuration and specific steel grade used. We provide detailed load charts with every installation.

Do you offer boltless racking for lighter storage?

Yes, we provide modular boltless racks which are easy to assemble and reconfigure, perfect for small parts storage, retail fulfillment, or e-commerce centers requiring high picking efficiency.

What safety features are included in your racking systems?

All our racks are OSHA-aligned, featuring safety pins, upright guards, and high-visibility finishes. We also ensure seismic stability and proper load labeling for every project.

Can you design multi-tier mezzanine floors?

Absolutely. We design and install structural and rack-supported mezzanine systems to effectively double or triple your vertical storage capacity, utilizing every cubic inch of your warehouse space.
