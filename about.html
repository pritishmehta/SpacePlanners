Our Story Mission & Values Why Choose Us Certifications Leadership Team

Space Planners India is India's leading manufacturer and supplier of premium storage systems. Since our founding in 2004, we have distinguished ourselves as pioneers in the storage solutions industry — delivering bespoke systems to India's most demanding clients.

We specialize in manufacturing high-quality mobile compactor storage systems, industrial lockers, storage racks, and comprehensive document management solutions across manufacturing units, corporate offices, government institutions, and healthcare facilities.

Our products are engineered with meticulous attention to detail — featuring easy maintenance, exceptional resistance against abrasion, rust, and burglary, longer service life, and seamless aesthetics.
