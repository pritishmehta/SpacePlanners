3. LAWFUL BASIS FOR DATA PROCESSING

Under GDPR Article 6:

  • Consent: Marketing emails, optional analytics, non-essential cookies

  • Legitimate Interest: Website improvement, fraud prevention, security

Under India IT Rules 2021:

  • Explicit Consent: All data collection requires clear opt-in

Under CCPA:

  • Limited use of sensitive personal information

5. YOUR RIGHTS (GDPR, CCPA, PIPEDA, India IT Rules)

🇪🇺 GDPR Rights (EU Residents):

  • Right to Access: Get copy of your data within 30 days
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: "Right to be forgotten" (with exceptions)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive data in machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of marketing, profiling
  • Rights Related to Automated Decision-Making: Human review available

🇺🇸 CCPA Rights (California Residents):

  • Right to Know: What personal info is collected
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of data
  • Right to Opt-Out: If we sell data (we don't)
  • Right to Correct: Fix inaccurate information
  • Right to Limit Use: Restrict data usage
  • Do Not Track (DNT): Honored across our website

🇨🇦 PIPEDA Rights (Canadian Residents):

  • Right to access personal information
  • Right to request correction
  • Right to know how data is used
  • Right to complaint to Privacy Commissioner

🇮🇳 India IT Rules 2021 Rights (Indian Residents):

  • Right to Access: Know what data we hold
  • Right to Correction: Update inaccurate data
  • Right to Deletion: Request data removal
  • Right to Grievance Redressal: File complaint with grievance officer
  • Right to Know Purpose: Understand why we collect data

🇧🇷 LGPD Rights (Brazilian Residents):

  • Right to confirmation of data processing
  • Right to access personal data
  • Right to data correction
  • Right to anonymized data
