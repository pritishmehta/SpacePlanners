Locker FAQ

Common Questions About Storage Lockers

Find answers about our secure and customizable locker systems.

What locking options are available for your lockers?

We offer a wide variety of security options including traditional key locks, digital keypad locks, RFID systems, and advanced biometric access for high-security environments.

Are your lockers suitable for healthcare and hospital environments?

Yes, we manufacture NABH-aligned lockers with antimicrobial coatings and integrated ventilation systems, specifically designed for staff and patient use in sterile hospital settings.

Can we customize the colors and sizes of the locker banks?

Definitely. We offer standard modular sizes as well as fully custom configurations. You can also choose from a wide range of RAL powder-coated colors to match your branding.

Are the lockers resistant to rust and heavy daily use?

Every locker is treated with a multi-stage anti-corrosion process and finished with high-quality powder coating to ensure long-term resistance to rust and wear in high-traffic areas.
