Project FAQ

Common Questions About Our Projects

Find answers about our execution, timelines, and project management process.

What is the typical timeline from inquiry to installation?

We provide a quote within 2 hours of inquiry. Depending on the scale and customization, production usually takes 2-4 weeks, followed by efficient on-site installation by our factory-trained teams.

Do you provide 3D layout designs before we commit to a project?

Absolutely. We offer complimentary on-site space assessments and detailed 3D CAD layouts so you can visualize the optimized storage density and workflow before any fabrication begins.

Are your projects covered by a comprehensive warranty?

Yes, all our projects come with a standard manufacturer's warranty. We also offer Annual Maintenance Contracts (AMC) to ensure your mobile systems and racks remain in peak operational condition for decades.

Can you handle large-scale pan-India installations?

With 2000+ installations across India, we have the logistics and technical capacity to execute projects in any major city, ensuring consistent quality and adherence to safety standards everywhere.
