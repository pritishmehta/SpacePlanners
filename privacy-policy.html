Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 5, 2026
Effective Date: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Space Planners India ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise process your personal data when you visit our website (https://spaceplannersindia.in) and interact with our services.

This Privacy Policy applies to all visitors and users of our website. By accessing and using our website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Collect

A. Information You Provide Directly:

  • Contact Forms: When you submit inquiry, demo request, or quote forms, we collect your name, email address, phone number, company name, and any messages you provide.
  • Communication: When you contact us via email, phone, or WhatsApp, we collect information you provide in your communication.
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect login credentials and profile information.

B. Information Collected Automatically:

  • Cookies & Tracking: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information about your browsing behavior, device type, IP address, and pages visited.
  • Analytics: Google Analytics helps us understand how users interact with our website (traffic sources, user behavior, conversions).
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type, and other technical information.

C. Information from Third Parties:

  • Google Ads and other advertising platforms may share information about your interactions with ads.

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: Process your inquiries, provide quotes, and deliver storage solution consultations.
  • Communication: Respond to your inquiries and contact you about your demo requests.
  • Marketing: Send you marketing emails, newsletters, and promotional updates (with your consent).
  • Website Improvement: Understand user behavior and optimize website functionality.
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfill legal obligations under India IT Rules 2021 and GDPR (where applicable).
  • Analytics: Track performance metrics and conversion data for Google Ads optimization.
  • Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent fraudulent activities.

4. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR & CCPA Compliance)

  • Consent: We process your data with your explicit consent (e.g., contact forms, marketing emails).
  • Legitimate Interest: We have legitimate interests in improving our services, marketing, and analytics.
  • Legal Obligation: We may process data to comply with applicable laws and regulations.
  • Contract Performance: We process data to fulfill service requests and agreements.

5. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We may share your personal data with:

  • Service Providers: Email hosting, analytics platforms (Google Analytics), CRM systems, and hosting providers.
  • Google Ads: Conversion data for campaign optimization (conversion tracking).
  • Government Authorities: If required by law or legal process.
  • Business Partners: We do not sell your data to third parties without explicit consent.

International Data Transfers: Your data may be transferred to countries outside India, including the EU and US. We implement appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, Privacy Shield) for such transfers.

6. Data Retention

  • Contact Inquiries: Retained for 2 years or until the business relationship ends.
  • Cookies: Session cookies expire when you close your browser; persistent cookies expire after 12 months.
  • Analytics Data: Google Analytics data retained for 26 months by default.
  • Marketing Lists: Retained until you unsubscribe or for 3 years, whichever comes first.

7. Your Rights & Choices

A. GDPR Rights (for EU residents):

  • Right of Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("Right to be Forgotten").
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how your data is used.
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of marketing communications and certain processing.

B. India IT Rules 2021 Rights (for India residents):

  • Right to Know: Know what data we collect and how we use it.
  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Right to Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion under applicable circumstances.

C. Marketing Opt-Out:

You can unsubscribe from marketing emails by clicking the "Unsubscribe" link in any email or contacting us directly.

D. Cookie Management:

You can manage cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect website functionality.

8. Security Measures

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal data:

  • SSL/TLS Encryption: Secure data transmission between your browser and our servers.
  • Access Controls: Only authorized personnel can access personal data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the data necessary for our purposes.

Note: While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

9. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

A. Cookie Types:

  • Necessary Cookies: Required for website functionality (session management, security).
  • Analytics Cookies: Google Analytics tracks user behavior and conversion metrics.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for Google Ads conversion tracking and remarketing.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember user preferences (dark mode, language).

B. Third-Party Services Using Cookies:

  • Google Analytics: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ads: https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Facebook Pixel: https://www.facebook.com/privacy/policy
