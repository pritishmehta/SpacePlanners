Industry FAQ

Common Questions About Industrial Applications

Find answers about how we tailor storage solutions for different specialized sectors.

How do you tailor storage solutions for the pharmaceutical sector?

We focus on GMP/WHO compliance, using non-corrosive materials (like SS-304/316) and designs that support FIFO (First-In, First-Out) inventory management and strict hygiene protocols.

What storage challenges do you solve for government offices?

We help government departments manage massive archival volumes with high-security lockable filing and motorized compactor systems that ensure sensitive document preservation and quick, indexed retrieval.

Can your warehouse racking systems integrate with digital WMS software?

Yes, our racking and mezzanine layouts are engineered to complement modern Warehouse Management Systems (WMS), optimizing picking paths, inventory visibility, and overall logistics throughput.

Do you provide space-saving solutions for educational institutions?

Yes, from high-density mobile library shelving to multi-tier student locker banks and administrative record storage, we provide durable, low-maintenance systems for schools and universities.
