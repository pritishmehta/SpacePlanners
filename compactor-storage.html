Compactor FAQ

Common Questions About Mobile Compactors

Find technical answers about our space-saving mobile storage systems.

How much floor space can a Mobile Compactor system save?

Our Mobile Compactor systems are designed to save up to 50-60% of floor space by eliminating multiple access aisles and utilizing a precision-engineered rail system that requires only one moving aisle.

Are the compactors safe for delicate archival or medical materials?

Yes, they feature anti-tamper locks, vibration-free movement, and can be customized with perforated panels for ventilation or stainless steel components for GMP-compliant medical storage.

What is the difference between manual and motorized compactors?

Manual compactors use an ergonomic hand-crank system for effortless movement, while motorized compactors offer push-button or remote access, ideal for high-frequency retrieval and large-scale industrial operations.

Is the rail system difficult to install on existing floors?

Our engineers specialize in seamless installation. We offer both floor-recessed tracks for a flush finish and surface-mounted tracks with ramps that integrate perfectly with your existing floor layout with minimal disruption.
