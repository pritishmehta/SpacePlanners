## 📋 REGULATORY REQUIREMENTS ADDRESSED

### ✅ GDPR (EU General Data Protection Regulation)
- **Scope:** Anyone from EU accessing the site
- **Requirements:**
  - Explicit consent before data collection
  - Right to access, delete, rectify data
  - Data processing lawful basis
  - Privacy by design
  - Data Breach Notification (72 hours)
  - Data Protection Officer contact

### ✅ CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
- **Scope:** California residents
- **Requirements:**
  - Right to know what data is collected
  - Right to delete
  - Right to opt-out of sale
  - Do Not Track (DNT) signals honored
  - Disclosure of data practices

### ✅ India IT Rules 2021
- **Scope:** All Indian users
- **Requirements:**
  - Clear privacy policy
  - Consent-based data collection
  - Grievance officer contact
  - Data processing transparency
  - 30-day response to grievances
  - No sensitive personal data without consent

### ✅ PIPEDA (Canada Personal Information Protection)
- Consent-based collection
- Information accessibility
- Accurate information maintenance

### ✅ LGPD (Brazil Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados)
- Purpose limitation
- Data necessity principle
- User consent management
